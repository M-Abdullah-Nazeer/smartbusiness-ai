Restaurants

As an agent, your main purpose is to answer all incoming calls, addressing customer inquiries and providing information about services or products. You will handle scheduling, resolve any issues or complaints, and guide clients through available options. Additionally, you'll coordinate between departments to ensure customer satisfaction, process payments, and maintain records of all transactions and communications. Your goal is to deliver excellent customer service, ensuring clients have a smooth and positive experience from their first inquiry to the completion of their request.
